Weekend Plans

While Saturday is the main event(and we hope to have everyone there for the midday ceremony, lunch, party, and supper!), the rest of the weekend is optional and designed to be flexible:

The Region

Our wedding will take place in beautiful area of northern Spain. The Pyrenees offer mountain views, rich history, and charming villages.

  • Isín is a restored, once-abandoned Pyrenean village. We’ll have it to ourselves!
  • Sabiñánigo (7 miles to Isín) is a small, convenient town with great transport connections.
  • Jaca (14 miles to Isín), slightly further, is a gem with medieval streets and lovely scenery.

Location

Getting here

The town of Sabiñánigo is accessible via train and coach/bus from Zaragoza, Huesca, and Pamplona (all of which are on main train lines).
From there, it’s a short drive to Isín by taxi —or we’ll gladly help coordinate lifts with local friends!
By car it’s about 3.5h from Barcelona and Bilbao, 1.5h from Zaragoza or a little over 4h from Madrid and Toulouse.
So for flights, consider Zaragoza, Bilbao, Barcelona, Madrid or even Toulouse as options.

For ferries consider Bilbao and Santander
  • Trains: Renfe
  • Buses: Alsa
  • Journey planning: Rome to Rio

Accommodation

We’ve booked the whole village! Basic on-site options include:

  • A youth hostel with shared rooms (4-8 beds). We’ll obviously keep families and friends together.
  • Apartments (3-11 people) reserved for older guests and families with younger kids.
This accommodation is a budget option at just 15€ per person/night —just let us know if you’d like to stay there.
Breakfast will also be included there, or you can make the most of the mornings and explore the area.

Other nearby options include hotels, B&Bs, campsites, cabins and apartments. Sabiñánigo is the closest, while Jaca offers a more scenic option.

What to wear

Although we're celebrating in the beautiful countryside, we want you to bring your A-game and dress to impress, while staying comfy.
Dress? Think elegant! This is your chance to dust off those ex-bridesmaid or prom dresses, bring out that dress you've been wanting to wear on a special occasion!
Shoes! Just remember, it's a bit hilly, so opt for flat or small heels to avoid any unplanned acrobatics.
Suit? We want you suited and booted, but don’t worry, tuxedos are not required. A nice suit will do the trick.
Temperature wise we expect it to be warm (around 20ºC average, max 25º), but to cool down in the evenings (min 13º).
Isín gets about 6 rainy days in September, on average, so we've got a good chance of dry weather.
